Our Services

Comprehensive 3D measurement and reconstruction solutions

3D Scanning & Measurement

Our advanced 3D scanning technology captures precise measurements of objects and environments with sub-millimeter accuracy. Using state-of-the-art optical and structured light systems, we deliver comprehensive digital representations suitable for analysis, documentation, and reconstruction.

  • Sub-millimeter precision
  • Full-color point cloud capture
  • High-speed data acquisition
  • Real-time visualization

3D Reconstruction

Transform raw 3D scan data into production-ready models using our specialized reconstruction algorithms. Our software processes point clouds, mesh generation, surface refinement, and texture mapping to create accurate digital replicas suitable for various applications including manufacturing, heritage preservation, and virtual environments.

  • Automated mesh generation
  • Surface optimization
  • Texture mapping and color correction
  • Multi-format export support
