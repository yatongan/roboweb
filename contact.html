Frequently Asked Questions

What file formats do you support?

We support all major 3D formats including OBJ, FBX, STL, PLY, GLTF, and many more. See our software page for a complete list.

Do you offer on-site installation and training?

Yes! We provide comprehensive installation, configuration, and training services for all our hardware and software solutions.

What level of accuracy can I expect?

Our hardware achieves sub-millimeter accuracy (±0.1mm absolute, ±0.05mm relative). Actual results depend on object size, material, and environmental conditions.

Can I integrate your API into my existing system?

Absolutely! We offer comprehensive API documentation and SDKs for Python, C++, and other languages. Our technical team can assist with custom integration.

Do you offer a free trial?

Yes! We offer 30-day free trials of our software platform. Hardware demos are also available by appointment.
